# Azi-Verse Story Evaluation Rubric

## Purpose

Evaluate Azi-Verse stories for developmental appropriateness, cultural authenticity, future-ready skill integration, universe consistency, and overall quality across target age groups (0-12 years).

## Rubric Selection Guide

- Chapter books (ages 7-10)

- Early readers with minimal illustrations

- Text-heavy narratives where visuals are decorative

- Board books (ages 0-3)

- Picture books (ages 4-8)

- Stories where illustrations carry narrative weight

## Standard Version (Text-Focused Stories)

| Criteria | Weight | Score Range | Description |

|----------|--------|-------------|-------------|

| **Age Appropriateness & Developmental Alignment** | 20% | 0-10 | Content matches cognitive, emotional, and language development of target age group (0-3, 4-8, or 7-10). Vocabulary, concepts, and themes developmentally appropriate. Avoids inappropriate content while remaining engaging. |

| **Story Structure & Narrative Quality** | 20% | 0-10 | Clear beginning, middle, end with age-appropriate complexity. Uses "inception-style" layering (surface entertainment + deeper learning). Logical event sequence. Gentle journey with quiet turning points. |

| **Cultural Authenticity & Multilingual Integration** | 15% | 0-10 | Respectful, accurate representation of Colombian and Indian cultures. Natural code-switching between Spanish, Hindi/Gujarati, and English (1-2 heritage words per page max). Avoids stereotypes. Family consultation evident. |

| **Language & Literary Elements** | 15% | 0-10 | Vocabulary enriching but accessible. Sentence structures suitable for age. Engaging literary devices enhance comprehension. Natural dialogue that reflects multilingual family patterns. |

| **Future-Ready Skills Integration** | 20% | 0-10 | Naturally embeds cognitive skills (critical thinking, AI literacy), emotional intelligence, adaptability, or other 21st-century competencies. Skills emerge from plot, not explicit teaching. Age-appropriate complexity. |

| **Universe Consistency & Character Development** | 10% | 0-10 | Characters consistent with established Azi-Verse personalities and relationships. World-building aligns with universe guide. Character growth feels natural and age-appropriate. Maintains continuity across stories. |

## Illustrated Version (Visual-Narrative Stories)

|----------|--------|-------------|-------------|

## Quality Indicators

### Excellent Azi-Verse Stories Demonstrate:

- **Seamless Cultural Integration:** Heritage languages feel natural, not forced

- **Character Authenticity:** Azi and companions behave consistently with established personalities

- **Skill Development:** Children learn 21st-century skills through engaging narrative

- **Emotional Resonance:** Stories connect with multicultural children's experiences

- **Interactive Potential:** Natural pause points for parent-child discussion and prediction

- **Therapeutic Value:** Characters model emotional regulation and problem-solving

- **Universal Appeal:** Specific cultural details create universal emotional connections

### Common Issues to Avoid:

- **Cultural Tokenism:** Using heritage languages or traditions superficially

- **Skill Preaching:** Explicitly teaching rather than embedding skills naturally

- **Character Inconsistency:** Contradicting established Azi-Verse personalities

- **Age Inappropriateness:** Content too advanced or too simple for target development

- **Forced Integration:** Awkward insertion of cultural or educational elements

- **Stereotypical Representation:** Relying on cultural clichs rather than authentic details

## Special Evaluation Considerations

### Interactive Elements Assessment:

- Natural "prediction pause" opportunities

- Parent-child discussion potential

- Whisker-wiggle roll call integration

- Cultural learning moments for families

### Therapeutic Value Review:

- Emotional regulation modeling

- Problem externalization opportunities

- Character coping strategies

- Comfort and security elements

### Cultural Consultation Requirements:

- Heritage language accuracy verification

- Cultural tradition authenticity check

- Community elder review completion

- Stereotype avoidance confirmation

## Final Score Calculation

1. Multiply each criterion score by its weight percentage

2. Sum all weighted scores for final rating

3. Document specific strengths and improvement areas

4. Provide actionable feedback aligned with Azi-Verse goals

- **90-100:** Ready for Azi-Verse publication

- **80-89:** High quality, minor cultural/skill integration refinements needed

- **70-79:** Good foundation, moderate improvements in authenticity or skill embedding required

- **60-69:** Acceptable basic story, significant development needed for universe standards

- **Below 60:** Substantial revision required before meeting Azi-Verse quality standards

## Documentation Requirements

For each evaluation, document:

- Target age band and format rationale

- Cultural authenticity verification process

- Future-ready skills identification and integration assessment

- Universe consistency check results

- Recommended next steps for improvement or approval
